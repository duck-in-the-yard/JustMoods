🟢 JustMoods is a minimalistic android app, to record, track and view your everyday mood. It gives you three daily tasks that help with keeping a healthy lifestyle 🟢
  
📋 CHECK DAILY TASKS  
    - There are three tasks to complete daily. A physical win (sports, walking etc.), a mental win (do something for your mental health e.g. meditation, journaling), a personal win (that can be anything you see as a personal win for yourself)  

➕ ADD YOUR EVERYDAY MOOD  
    - On the "Add Mood" tab you can add the mood for the current day choosing from five options (great, nice, okay, bad, very bad). Adding the mood for past days is not possible (more about this in the next tab)  
    - The calendar is scrollable to see the past months. Tap and hold on the calendar to get back to the current month.

📊 OVERVIEW OF YOUR DATA  
    - You can check the distribution of you mood data over the current year (january 1st to december 31st) and of all time  
    - You can also see the streak here. Streaks are counted by the consecutive days you recorded a mood. The current streak and your best streak of all time will be displayed  

⚙️ SETTINGS  
    - Export your mood data as JSON file  
    - Import your mood data  
    - Delete all data  
    - Change the name that will be displayed on the home screen  
    - Switch between light and dark mode  

🔭 PLANNED FEATURES FOR THE FUTURE

    - Mood Average statistic  
    - Sleep Tracker
